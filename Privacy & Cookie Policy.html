We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 18. If we become aware that a child has provided us with personal data, we will take steps to delete such information immediately. If you believe we might have any information from a child, please contact us.

16. Remove Your Account & Personal Data

You have the right to request the deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

  • How to Delete: You can initiate account deletion directly within the App by navigating to Settings > Delete Account. Alternatively, you may contact us at privacy@Telstraight.com.
  • What Happens: Upon request, your profile, portfolio, and personal data will be permanently deleted from our active databases within 30 days.
  • Exceptions: We may retain certain information (such as transaction history and tax records for affiliate payouts) as required by law or for legitimate business purposes (e.g., fraud prevention).
